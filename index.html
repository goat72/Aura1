Server Rules

We keep Shadow positive and fair. Read these before joining—violations may lead to warnings or bans.

Treat all members with kindness. No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or toxicity based on race, gender, religion, etc.

Don't flood channels with messages, links, or self-promotion. Use designated channels for shares, and get mod approval for ads.

No NSFW, gore, or explicit content. This is a family-friendly server—keep it clean.

Stay on-topic in channels. Use voice chat respectfully, and report issues to mods privately.
